## 🎯 About the Project

Evalua is designed to be a dynamic and engaging **electronic school platform** focused on mathematics education, featuring interactive lessons on **Trigonometry**, **Geometry**, **Algebra**, **Calculus**, and **Combinatorics**.

With its rich content including practice exercises, homework tasks, and customizable tests drawn from categorized question banks, the platform not only assesses knowledge but also builds problem-solving skills through English-language instruction.

With its systematic approach, teacher-validated materials, and detailed statistics on performance, Evalua is well-positioned to deliver a meaningful and effective learning experience that aligns with goals of mathematical mastery and intellectual growth.
